New Staff Onboarding Process

Current P2PU staffers will:
  • Give you an @p2pu.org mailing address
  • (If applicable) - Give you the twitter username/password
  • Will point you to the places where:
    • discussions happen: lists.p2pu.org
      • Community mailing list - http://groups.google.com/group/p2pu-community
    • people hang out: IRC/gchat
    • users get help: help.p2pu.org
  • Will introduce you to staff you haven't met yet (http://pad.p2pu.org/p/community-responsibilities):
    • Philipp, Executive Director, Skype: iphilipp, Twitter: @sharingnicely, GTalk philipp@p2pu.org
    • Bekka - General admin & communications person Skype: rebekahn, GTalk & email: bekka@p2pu.org
    • Vanessa - Learning Lead - twitter: @mozzadrella, GTalk & email: Vanessa@p2pu.org

In your first week at P2PU:
  • Brush up your profile at p2pu.org
  • Join the following P2PU mailing lists (lists.p2pu.org)
    • community - http://groups.google.com/group/p2pu-community
    • dev - http://lists.p2pu.org/mailman/listinfo/p2pu-dev
    • webcraft (often important messages come through here) - http://lists.p2pu.org/mailman/listinfo/p2pu-webcraft
  • Send introduction to the community list
    • Also post on blog
  • Get added to the people page on info.p2pu.org (point of contact: Bekka)
    • photo that catches your whole face without cutting you off
    • looking mostly at the camera would be best
    • 500 x 500px is large enough, a bit smaller could be ok too
    • background shouldn't be too noisy, either a plain background or something that was on purpose (ie not your kitchen)
  • Check out the TRELLO boards for dev projects and org work
    • https://trello.com/board/project-pipeline/4ec0f020c137ff072a5d8afa
    • https://trello.com/board/p2pu-org/4eaf963a6093a2544d9936b8
  • Create a persoal work board on TRELLO
  • Join the community call -  http://pad.p2pu.org/community-call
    • First week we will do intros with everyone on the call (see Chloe's comment below)
  • Add your schedule to the P2PU calendar
  • Acquaint with P2PU etherpad search: http://padsearch.p2pu.org
    • We LOVE etherpads for planning and collaborative note taking
  • Speak to Bekka about setting up your banking details in our electronic banking system and our payment cycles (if you're a contractor)

In the first month:
  • Participate in a P2PU course
    • blog about it
  • Join the community calls: http://pad.p2pu.org/community-call [moved this to first week]
  • Write a post for the P2PU blog about your work
  • Get an idea of some of our (sometimes unusual) processes - ie: the "rough consensus" idea for making decisions among the community
