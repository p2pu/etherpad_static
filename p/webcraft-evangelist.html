Webcraft Evangelist

About the Job

Do you believe everyone should know how the web works? Do you want to show hungry minds the way to enlightenment? P2PU is seeking a creatively minded web developer with a passion for teaching web skills to encourage and build a peer learning community of web makers. 

See "Apply" below for application details.

About P2PU - Why we're here and how we're changing the world:

  • P2PU is an online community for learning anything with your peers. We're recognized as a 501(c)3 non-profit public charity in the United States.
  • Over 20,000 individuals use P2PU. Our community is global, multi-lingual, and incredibly passionate about learning.
  • We're funded by [respected foundations](/supporters) and [top industry partners](/partners). We have a [great staff](/staff) supporting an [amazing community of volunteers](/showcase).
  • P2PU [values](/values) openness, community, and peer learning. We're changing the way people learn from each other online.

  • 100% Open Source - We open source all of our code, in fact we require it. Check out our [Github](http://github.com/p2pu).
  • We're changing the world. 
  • We embrace transparency - You can view our [project pipeline](https://trello.com/board/project-pipeline/4ec0f020c137ff072a5d8afa), read our [emails](https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/p2pu-community), and [contribute](/contribute) as a volunteer.

Apply - What to include in your application and where to send it:
To apply, send the following to jobs@p2pu.org in a single PDF. "Webcraft Evangelist" should be the subject line.
  • Cover Letter including
    • Why you want this job.
    • A link to your Github profile or a link to some of your favorite code you've written.
    • Links to applications you've built that are currently online.
    • What you would learn if you could learn anything.
  • Resume
