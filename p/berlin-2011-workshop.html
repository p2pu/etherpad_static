Miscellaneous Notes:
*P2PU - Make smart choices
*Community/P2PU as a social equalizer. ie. when you walk into any other environment that is not a learning environment, there are automatic assumptions and judgments made. imagine walking into a bar, into a company interview, into a party. there are social proclivities that automatically get manifested in certain behaviors that may isolate or polarize. in a learning environment, such as within a university lecture hall, people enter on the same footing, and stereotypes/preconceptions/beliefs are suspended for a time. how to translate to online environemnt/how to express that this is a benefit of P2PU?
