1. Introductions + Overview - 30 min
  • Class of 2012
    • What aspect of openness do you want to learn with others? to teach others?
    • Name a time when openness helped you.
  • Overview, Q&A 
2. Teach someone something with open content - 1.5 hours
  • In groups of 2-4 take this challenge together: https://p2pu.org/en/groups/teach-someone-something-with-open-content/
    • 1) Choose a topic according to http://pad.p2pu.org/p/school-of-open-guidelines
    • 2) Find 3 open resources - take notes below or create a new pad at http://pad.p2pu.org
    • 3) Round-robin teaching 
3. Make a course in 30 min - 1 hour
  • Turn what you taught into a P2PU course: https://p2pu.org/en/groups/make-a-course/
    • Register for a P2PU account and start creating: https://p2pu.org/en/groups/create/
    • Join the discussion list and introduce yourself: https://groups.google.com/group/school-of-open. Send a note when you're ready for feedback.
  • Closing circle
