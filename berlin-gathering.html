Hotel Options:

  • Real Berlin -> http://www.michelbergerhotel.com/ - highly recommended by my friend. this will be a real slice of berlin. Between Friedrichshain and Kreuzberg.
  • Safe choice -> http://www.lux-eleven.com/ - nice boutique hotel (from ~ 110 EUR / room) in Mitte
  • Safe choice -> http://www.the-weinmeister.com/ - trendy boutique hotel (about the same as lux) in Mitte
  • http://www.huettenpalast.de/ - three types of rooms (cabines, trailers, rooms). only rooms have individual baths. cheap and in neukoelln which is the hippest neighborhood

Neighborhoods:

  • Prenzlauer Berg - Old East, beautiful big and renovated buildings, grand avenues, the iconic image of the old Berlin. A little boring. 
  • Mitte - The busy cousin to Prenzlauer Berg. Not as beautiful, but more happening. 
  • Neukoelln - The current cool kid on the block.
  • Kreuzberg - Leftist enclave during the 80s, now a good mix of old and new

Restaurants:

  • Cookies Cream - very stylish and amazing vegetarian (not what you think). This is a must visit!
