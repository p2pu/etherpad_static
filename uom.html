Potential Research Questions to Explore
  * What factors motivate learners' participation/contribution to P2PU courses? (characteristics of the course design? other group contributions? individual history or characteristics?)
  * How do participation patterns (e.g. members taking more than one course) relate to future participation/contribution?
  * From Philipp/P2PU - we have anecdotal evidence that participation in courses drops off after 2-3 weeks, is this the case, and why?  What could we understand about this phenomena?
  * Can we link members' participation in P2PU with measures of 21st Century Literacy Skills?  e.g. Does participating in a peer to peer learning environment foster new literacy skills?
