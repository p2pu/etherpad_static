Why are we doing this?
(other than video +learning being massively popular and succseful? kahn academy, ted, instructables)
Well alot of the assessment work we are doing talks about evidence centered design > showing to others how you make sense of a concept as a way to better understand it. I think video tutorials are a very popular medium and p2pu would benefit from having some. And our challenge could seed new challenges that include videos...
