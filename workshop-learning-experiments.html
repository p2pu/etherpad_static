  • Present a prototype (extra points for prototypes that are live on the site)
  • Be experimental. Push the boundaries of what P2PU could be regarding:
    • subject matter ("a course on XYZ? no way!" "yes way!") 
    • structure ("we all meditate together at the same time")
    • impact ("i'm going to teach 1M kids in Uganda to read by Nov 15 2011")
  • Joint experimentation with other participant is encouraged (and less work)
  • Make sure to mention any ideas you have for the platform that will help you run this
