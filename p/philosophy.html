Introduction to Philosophy:

Course Description:
An introduction to Philosophy through the practice of questioning, reflection & diaolouge. 

Possible topics: 

  • What is Art?
  • What is the Good?
  • Appearance & Reality
  • Sexual & Relatonal Ethics
  • Survey peers for topics for last few meetings

Format:

  • Work through some texts, offer help pro-actively & during set "office hours"
  • Use online meetings to talk about texts
  • Use online meetings to try and practice answering some philosophical questions
  • Final project: short paper on a topic, distribute to other peers, use last meeting as a discussion on short paper topic
  • Try to meet once a week via skype or web-conf
  • Try to match texts to what can be reasonably done in a week
