Communication Plan

For Learners & Mentors:

  • WHY? What's the motive? Why should I start or join a study group?
  • HOW? How do I do it? What are the steps in the process?

For Us

Theory and Plan

1) Convert traffic to sign-ups
2) Convert sign-ups into STARTING or JOINING study groups, and inviting their friends
3) People in those study groups create questions and complete learning tasks
4) That lead to assessments and badges
5) Those people now have more credibility and social capital. They become leaders and mentors who create new study groups. In a cyclical process that scales.

Revised Metrics

Original roadmap

Q1 - 1000 learners (Courses: Opportunity-driven)
Q2 - 2000 learners (Courses: Prioritization)
Q3 - 4000 learners (Courses: Diversity & Excellence)
Q4 - 10000 learners (Courses: Full spectrum)

Why Re-Boot?

  • Challenges to original model:
    • Instructor led: Instructors don't scale as easily as demand
    • Fixed course cycles: create long stretches where no courses are open for sign-up
  • Solutions:
    • Strengthen incentives to facilitators (reputation benefits)
    • Lower barriers to participation (learners and facilitators) by allowing more flexible "study groups" in addition to courses
    • Allow courses and groups to start at any time, not tied to fixed cycles

Re-boot

  • Platform: Build the more flexible model into Lernanta (based on Batucada)
  • Support: Provide support scaffolding, helpdesk
  • Promotion: Strengthen social media promotion for individual courses and groups

Implications for original roadmap:

  • Lower the goals for Q2 (number of overall users still ok, but active learners lower)
  • New model is designed to allow faster scaling of community
  • Consider both peripheral (low activity = "user") and active learners in growth planning
  • Expect to be back on track by end Q3, and hit similar level of ambition as intended for Q4
